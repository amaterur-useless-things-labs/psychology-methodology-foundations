## Course Overview

**Title:** Methodological Foundations of Psychology: Scientific Version  
**Subtitle:** "The foundations on which stands a building where everyone argues whether it is a building at all"

**Course Goals:**
- Critically evaluate psychological research from a methodological perspective
- Distinguish between scientific and non-scientific approaches in psychology
- Understand the structure of scientific knowledge in psychology
- Choose and justify research methods
- Understand the epistemological foundations of various psychological schools
- See connections between theory, method, and data
- Apply methodological knowledge in their own research practice
